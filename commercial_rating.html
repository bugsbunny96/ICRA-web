INTERNATIONAL CREDIT RATING AGENCY (ICRA) LLC

PROCESS FOR COMMERCIAL CREDIT RATING

1- What is Credit Rating


Credit rating is assessment of the creditworthiness of a borrower in general terms or with respect to a financial obligation. A credit rating is assigned to any entity that wants to borrow money. It can be an individual, a corporation, a state or a sovereign government.

2- What is a Credit Rating Agency (CRA)


A credit rating agency is a company that assesses the financial strength of companies and government entities, especially their ability to meet principal and interest payments on their debts. The rating assigned to a given entity shows an agency’s level of confidence that the borrower will honor its debt obligations as agreed.

3- Process of Credit Rating


Credit rating process is the process in which a credit rating agency takes details of an entity and analyses it so that everyone else can use those ratings. In other words, it is an assessment of the borrower’s ability to repay his or her financial obligations and the creditworthiness of an individual, organization, etc. can be evaluated by taking various factors into due consideration that represents the willingness and capability of a borrower to timely discharge his/her financial commitment.

4- Why companies opt for Credit Rating


a- Better decision making
No bank or money lender Companies would like to give money to a risky company. With Credit Rating, they can get better idea about the repayment capacity of that company so credit rating helps banks and money lenders to take better Investment decisions.

b- Safety Assured
High Credit rating means assurance about the safety of the money that it will be paid back with interest on time.

c- Easy Loan Approval
With a high rating the Company seen as a risk-free company so It gets easy for the company to get loan approval. Hence, lenders are more comfortable while making decisions.

d- Discounted rate of Interest
Credit rating helps borrowing Company to get debt at less rate of interest because one of the major factors that determine the rate of Interest on debt that is the credit rating. Higher the rating, lower the Interest or vice versa.

5- Rating Grades and their meaning


ICRA will adopt the following rating scales while assigning final rating to commercial entities.

# Points Range ICRA RATING MEANING
1 46 - 50 AA EXCELLENT
2 40 - 45 A STRONG
3 30 - 39 B GOOD
4 20 - 29 C AVERAGE
5 0 - 19 D POOR

6- Steps involved in Credit Rating Process at ICRA LLC


a- Receiving formal request from Client
The credit rating process begins when the issuer (who wants to get a credit rating) issues the formal request for credit rating to ICRA LLC along with necessary documents. When ICRA accepts the request, an agreement is made between the issuer company and ICRA LLC and the customer will pay the required fee to ICRA.

b- Assigning to Credit Risk Team
In the second step, ICRA assigns the job and duties of credit rating to Credit Risk team who is responsible for rating projects/assignments and have expertise in the relevant business area.

c- Obtaining financial information
Credit Risk team will collect all the required information of the client relating to its financial statements and other relevant information from the given documents.

d- Research and financial statement analysis
In this step, Credit Risk team research client’s operations, validate KYC documents, review the credit repayment history as per the given documents, analyze financial statements and cash flow projections. Credit risk team may communicate with the client for better understanding of the business model and future business prospects and may ask for additional documents and information whenever it deems necessary.

e- Discussion meeting
When all the analysis has been done, Credit Risk team will discuss the findings with the Board of Governors/Rating Committee, comprising senior management of ICRA LLC. All the issues having an effect on the company are identified. An opinion on the rating is also formed and the results of the team’s analysis are finally presented to the Board of Governors/Rating Committee.

f- Rating Committee meeting
This is the final authority meeting for assigning ratings and the work of the Credit Risk team comes to end. Now final authority will check facts, findings, and factors like political, social, and other factors. After considering all the results, facts, and other information the ICRA LLC will assign the actual Credit Rating.

g- Communication of decision
The assigned rating grade is communicated finally to the client along with reasons or rationale supporting the credit rating. The credit ratings which are not accepted are either rejected or reviewed in the light of additional facts provided by the client.
